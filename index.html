Who am I?

I’m a passionate IT CMS (Card Management System) and Switching in banking industry with a strong background in banking technology and development.

Currently, I work at Cathay United Bank Cambodia, specializing in the Card Management System (CMS) that powers secure and efficient card services.

Here’s a quick overview of my journey:
• 2022 : Joined the bank and starting my career in the CMS team. • 2023 : Officer, handling transaction flow analysis and card operations support. • 2024 : Senior Officer, responsible for advanced troubleshooting, system optimization, and security enhancements. • 2025 : Specialist, focusing on CMS architecture, ISO8583 message flows, PCI DSS compliance, SQL database management, and high-level technical support.

Throughout my time at the bank, I have developed strong knowledge in:

  • ISO8583 Transaction Message Handling
  • PCI DSS Card Security Standards
  • Transaction Flow Management
  • Troubleshooting Critical Card Issues
  • SQL Database Querying and Management

Alongside my banking role, I am passionate about Web Application Development.
I enjoy creating beautiful, functional websites and applications using modern technologies, delivering user-centric digital experiences.

With a Computer Science background and hands-on technical experience, I combine financial system mastery with creative digital solutions to drive success in every project.
